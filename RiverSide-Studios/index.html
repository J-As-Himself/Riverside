THE EXPERIENCE

Hello and welcome! We are RiverSide Studio - your Number One photography and videography company (wink wink). If there’s one thing we LOVE in this world - that is meeting new people and creating lifelong memories for our customers. We’re here to turn your photos into real life stories… Riverside Studio as a company provide you with wedding photographers and filmmakers, we’re never stuck in a “comfort zone” with our work. We ALWAYS strive to go above and beyond to create magic for our customers and because we put our 150% into our craft, we ask you to trust us and our creativity. We spend countless hours researching the best spots, time, light, composition and SO much more that will all add up to your life story so be assured that your wedding photos and film will be one of a kind.

IT’S ABOUT CAPTURING THE SOFT TOUCHES, WARM HUGS, LAUGHS AND TEARY EYES

When we first work with our clients, for instance during your shoot, you will probably see us doing a lot of talking and guiding with poses. This is simply because it’s the first time we work together and it’s a way for us to get comfortable around each other. You will see that as you become “experienced”, you will quickly feel at ease and get comfortable around us. This is where we then let you be yourselves and do fun things together whilst we document the moments. We love making you feel comfortable so you can forget our existence and be yourselves. This way, you will not only get the BEST photographs, but also have a good laugh and forget that your photos are being taken whilst you’re having FUN. Whilst we care about our crafts to the core of our beings, we don’t take life too seriously and are always up for a laugh But trust us, we’ll be there to celebrate you and document every little moment for you.
